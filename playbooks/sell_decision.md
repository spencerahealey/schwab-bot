# Playbook: Sell Decision

Triggered by: "Should I sell [TICKER]?", "thinking about exiting [TICKER]", "is it time to cut [TICKER]?", etc.

## Steps

1. **Pull thesis.** Read the position's thesis from `position_theses.md`.
2. **Research.** Current fundamentals, recent news, earnings, competitive shifts.
3. **Thesis evaluation.** Intact? Weakening? Broken? Be honest.
4. **Tax check.** If brokerage: short-term vs long-term gains, estimated tax impact. If IRA: tax-free, note that.
5. **Recommendation:**
   - **If thesis broken:** recommend exit, suggest where to redeploy proceeds
   - **If thesis weakening:** recommend trim or watch with specific triggers for exit
   - **If thesis intact but position is down:** push back on panic-selling, explain why
   - **If thesis intact but position is overweight:** discuss trimming for risk management
6. **If exiting:** move position to Closed Positions in `position_theses.md` with exit date and reason.

## Rules
- Cutting losses on a broken thesis is discipline, not panic.
- Holding through a dip on an intact thesis is also discipline.
- Always have a plan for the proceeds before selling.
