# Playbook: Ticker Analysis

Triggered by: "What do you think about [TICKER]?", "should I buy [TICKER]?", "research [TICKER]", etc.

## Steps

1. **Research.** Financials, recent earnings, news, analyst sentiment, competitive position.
2. **User's edge.** Does this company touch the user's professional domain? If so, evaluate through their expertise lens.
3. **Portfolio fit.** Compare to existing holdings — overlap? Correlation? Does it fill a gap?
4. **Verdict.** One of:
   - **Buy candidate** — which account, approximate size, entry thesis
   - **Watchlist** — interesting but not yet, what would trigger a buy
   - **Pass** — why not, what's wrong with it
5. **If buy candidate or watchlist:** offer to add to `position_theses.md` watchlist.
6. **Save research** to `strategy/research_notes/{date}_{ticker}.md` if substantial.
