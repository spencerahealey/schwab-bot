# Playbook: New Money Deployment

Triggered by: "I just invested $X", "where should I put $X", "I have cash to deploy", etc.

## Steps

1. **Clarify account.** Ask which account the money is in if not stated.
2. **Load state.** Read `portfolio_state.json` for current allocation and cash.
3. **Quick macro check.** Web search for current market conditions — not a full review, just enough context to inform timing.
4. **Review theses.** Read `position_theses.md` — which positions have the strongest conviction right now?
5. **Evaluate options:**
   - Add to existing high-conviction positions that are underweight relative to conviction?
   - Initiate a watchlist candidate?
   - Hold cash if market conditions warrant patience?
6. **Recommend.** Specific tickers, dollar amounts, reasoning. Be concrete — not "consider adding to semis" but "put $1,000 into NVDA because [reason]."
7. **Roth check.** If Roth IRA isn't maxed for the year ($7,000), mention it.

## Rules
- Never recommend buying something without a thesis.
- If the money should sit in cash, say so — deploying capital isn't always the right move.
- Factor in what's already concentrated before adding more.
